
Global Tech Inc. - Corporate Travel and Expense Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Department: Finance & Operations

1. Overview

This document outlines the permitted expenses for employees traveling on official company business. All expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the transaction date.

2. Transportation & Ride-Shares

Flights: Employees must book economy class for all flights under 6 hours. Business class is permitted for international flights exceeding 6 hours.

Ride-Shares (Uber/Lyft) & Taxis:

Ride-shares are fully reimbursable for travel between the airport, hotel, and client sites.

For late-night work at the home office (after 8:00 PM), employees are allowed a ride-share stipend of up to $50.00 per ride to get home safely.

Premium ride tiers (e.g., Uber Black) are strictly prohibited and will not be reimbursed.

3. Meals and Entertainment (M&E)

Daily Per Diem: Employees are granted a maximum daily meal allowance of $75.00 while traveling.

Client Dinners: Client entertainment meals are capped at $150.00 per person, including tax and tip. Alcohol is permitted but limited to two drinks per person.

4. Receipt Requirements

Itemized Receipts: A detailed, itemized receipt is required for ALL transactions exceeding $25.00.

Credit Card Statements: A credit card statement alone is not sufficient proof of purchase; the original vendor receipt showing line items must be provided.

Missing Receipts: If a receipt is lost, the employee must submit a "Missing Receipt Affidavit" signed by their direct manager.

5. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following items will not be reimbursed under any circumstances:

Parking tickets or traffic violations.

Personal grooming services (haircuts, spa treatments).

Mini-bar charges at hotels.
